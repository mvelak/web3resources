Web3.Career

The largest Web3 job board and talent marketplace.

What it is

Web3.Career is a job board and talent marketplace dedicated exclusively to blockchain, smart contract, and crypto positions, listing over 73,000 blockchain jobs at more than 7,000 Web3 projects.

What's in it

The platform covers the full spectrum of web3 employment: technical roles such as Solidity and Rust developers, smart contract engineers, and DevOps, alongside non-technical positions in marketing, community management, legal, and product management. It also includes developer salary data updated hourly, breakdowns by role, seniority, blockchain ecosystem, and location, plus a "Learn Web3" section featuring bootcamps, courses, and tutorials organized by skill level from beginner to advanced.

Relevance for Web3

Whether you are transitioning into the web3 workforce, benchmarking compensation before negotiating an offer, or hiring talent for a DeFi protocol or NFT platform, Web3.Career is purpose-built for those needs. It provides specialists with empirical data on market demand for specific skillsets (e.g., zero-knowledge proofs or Layer-2 scaling), making it an essential career planning tool.

Tips for Efficient Use

Use the multi-filter system combining role type, blockchain, seniority, and compensation range to narrow listings quickly. For career changers, the "Learn Web3" section is well-organized by difficulty level. Set up email alerts for your specific skill tags (e.g., "zero-knowledge + remote") so you do not need to check manually.
